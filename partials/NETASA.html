The New England Teacher at Sea Alumni (NETASA) network is comprised of NOAA Teacher at Sea Alumni from Connecticut, Massachusetts, Maine, New Hampshire, Rhode Island, and Vermont. Its goals are to build an understanding of physical and marine oceanography among teachers and students, establish strong working relationships among teachers that emphasize collective participation of groups of teachers and develop relationships among NOAA scientists, teachers, and students.
