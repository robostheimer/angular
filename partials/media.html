Over the years our teachers have taken and uploaded many stunning photos. The Teacher at Sea Media Library is an online vault where a all photos and videos uploaded by our teachers are stored and available for searchable access and download. All photos and videos are open for downloading and reuse with attribution to the NOAA Teacher at Sea Program

This database is browsable from this page by clicking on the teacher name in the yellow box in the right column. Additionally, the photo database is searchable from all pages by clicking on the search icon in the upper right portion of the Teacher at Sea website.

If you are interested in staff-curated photos, check out the Teacher at Sea Photo of the Week page (which contains over 100 photos and is searchable). and/or Did You Know: Tweets From Sea
